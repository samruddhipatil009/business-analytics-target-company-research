# Business Analytics Target Company Research

This repository contains my submission for the DeepThought Business Analytics Internship Assignment.

## Repository Contents

- sample-output.csv
- methodology.md
- code.md
- proposal-1000-companies.md

## Objective

Research 25 companies matching DeepThought's "Federer Company" Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) by evaluating:

- Producer Status
- Accessibility
- Differentiation
- Decision Maker Quality
- Growth Signals
- Systems Maturity
- Leadership Succession

Each company has been evaluated using publicly available information and scored according to the assignment guidelines.
